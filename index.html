About the seminar series

If you looking for an online seminar for latest updates on security & privacy research in Machine learning, then you come to the right place. Every Monday at UTC-12 5pm, we aim to host a talk and discussion from leading reseachers in the community. In paritcular, each session will be splitted into a talk (40 mins) followed by a Q&A and short discussion (20 mins). We hope that this seminar will further disseminate information about challenges and opportunities in security and privacy research in machine learning.
