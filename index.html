About the seminar series

The motivation for the seminar is to build a platform to discuss and disseminate the progress made by the community in solving some of the key challenges. We intend to host weekly talks from leading researchers in both academia and industry. Each session will be split into a talk (40 mins) followed by a Q&A + short discussion session (20 mins).

Timing: Every Tuesday at 1pm Eastern Time (Virtual talks)

We recommend following two steps to get details about talks (including zoom links):

  • Join the mailing list (here) to receive regular updates about talks.
  • Sync SPML calendar (here) to never miss a talk.
